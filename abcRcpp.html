“Rejection sampling”

We want the posterior: \(P(\theta| x)\). We can sample the joint pretty easily, \(P(\theta, x) = P(\theta) P(x | \theta)\). Now just constrain the \(P(\theta, x)\) samples to those where \(x = x_{obs}\).

Algorithm

  1. Sample \(\theta^*\) from the prior: \(P(\theta)\)
  2. Sample \(x^*\) from the likelihood: \(P(x|\theta^*)\)
  3. If \(x^*\) is equal to the observed data, keep \(\theta^*\), if not reject.
